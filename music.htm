Looping


Looping is a form of building a composition by a series of "loops" or single measure frames containing various or multiple elements, such as; drum tracks, bass tracks, ect. Some of my looping works also contain live overdubs as well as custom loops I made with other software. I used Sonic Foundry's ACID mainly for the compositions in this section.
